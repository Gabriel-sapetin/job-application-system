Safety Guide

Protecting Yourself on JobTrack

⚠️ Common Scam Warning Signs
  • Asked to pay a processing fee, training fee, or deposit before starting
  • Employer asks for your bank account, GCash, or government ID upfront
  • Job offers very high pay for little work with no interview
  • Assigned a "test task" then ghosted without payment
  • Communication moves to personal WhatsApp/Telegram immediately
  • Sent a check and asked to forward part of it — it will bounce
👤 For Individual / Odd Job Hirers
  • Meet in a public place first — mall, café, or barangay hall
  • Verify identity — ask for a valid ID before work begins
  • Never send payment in advance — pay upon completion only
  • Do not share your home address until identity is confirmed
  • Tell someone where you're going and who you're meeting
  • For domestic/home work, request a barangay clearance
